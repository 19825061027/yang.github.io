#+TITLE: 做一个专注点，C-x w标记当前位置为专注点, C-x C-w返回专注点 

通过counsel-bookmark找到类似代码：
(defvar me/bookmark-var "me/bookmark-var")
(defun me/bookmark-set nil
  (interactive)
  (bookmark-set me/bookmark-var))
(defun me/bookmark-jump nil
  (interactive)
  (bookmark-jump me/bookmark-var))
